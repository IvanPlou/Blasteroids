Blasteroids (Unity1_A2)

Controls:
Move forward: W / Up Arrow
Move back: S / Down Arrow
Move right: D / Right Arrow
Move left: A / Left Arrow
Aim: Move mouse
Shoot Laser: Mouse left click
Shoot Scatter Gun: Mouse right click

New feature: 
Electric Cannon: Mouse wheel (middle button) --> Bigger projectile that rotates and makes a differnt sound effect.
Cooldown and rotation adjustable in the editor.
